Top 3 Ambidextorous Mice:

Best Lightweight option:

I chose the Finalmouse ULX as the best lightweight option because it has the best wireless performance compared to the compition. It also is one of the few full sized mice that is under 40 grams.

Finalmouse ULX

Best readily available option:

I chose the pulsar X2 as the best readily available option beacuse it can be purchased from multiple retailers and is high performing. It is also under 60 grams. Another benefit is that there is no software required to change dpi.

Pulsar X2

Best Budget Option:

I chose the Razer Viper Mini as the best budget option because it has solid performance and is easy to obtain.

Razer Viper Mini

Top 3 Ergonomic Mice:

Best ergonomic mouse for productivity:

I chose the Logitech MX master 3S as the best ergo mouse for productivity because it has some of the best macro buttons on a mouse. It also has good connectivity options.

Logitech MX Master 3S

Best ergonomic mouse for gaming:

I chose the Pulsar Xlite V3 as the best ergo mouse for gaming because of it's good latency and weight.

Pulsar Xlite

Best budget ergonomic mouse:

I chose the Razer Deathadder Essential becasue it is a decent option for under $50. Theres nothing outstanding about it but also nothing to make it a dealbreaker.

Razer Deathadder Essential
